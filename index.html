Welcome to the world of Witcher Gwent!

What is Gwent and what cards are there?

For starters, let's touch on the basics. The card game Gwent is the successor to the dice game that made the Geralts happy in previous parts of the game universe. For those who are familiar with both the book universe and the game one, they know that the book version of Gwent has little in common with the game one, if only because there are four games at the table (instead of two) in the book, the cards look like a normal poker deck, and the very essence of the game is more like the usual poker than the collectible from the game. But, perhaps, this makes The Witcher 3 Gwent a more interesting interpretation of Sapkowski's creation. Before you start searching for a map, you should familiarize yourself with their types and characteristics, which is what you should do next.

Types of cards

In TheWitcher 3, taking into account all the released add-ons, there are 6 types of card decks, each of which has its own leaders with a certain set of skills. In addition to leaders, unit cards occupy a special place in the game (since their minimum number for a party must be at least 22 pieces). Units are divided into three categories - melee, ranged and siege. It is also worth noting the presence of neutral cards that are not tied to factions, including special cards. All this will be discussed further.

Where and how to find cards for Gwent?

Considering this issue, it is worth repeating that if you set yourself the goal of finding all the existing cards for gwent, and, as can be seen from the tables presented, there are more than 200 pieces, maximum attention should be paid to solving this problem from the very beginning of the game. Cards can be obtained in various ways: they can be found in chests, they can be won, they can be bought, they can be obtained by completing quests.

Bestiary

UIY

Deathwish: Shuffle Viy back to your deck then increase its base power by 3.

FUCUSIA

Deploy: Play a Skellige unit from your graveyard with a provision cost of 10 or less and give it Doomed, then Spawn Rain on the opposite row with a duration equal to unused provision.

PROFESSOR

Deploy: Put a Bounty on an enemy unit and damage it by 4. Tribute 3: Ignore a target's Armor.

GERALT OF RIVIA

Deploy: Destroy an enemy unit with 9 or more power.

THE MUSHY TRUFFLE

Resilience.
Deploy: Spawn and play a Bonded unit from your starting deck.
Order: Spawn and play Golden Froth.

REYNARD ODO

Formation.
Order: Boost adjacent units by 1.
Whenever you play a unit boost it by 1.
